無妄 Wú Wàng / Innocence (The Unexpected)

above:Qián The Creative, Heaven
below:Zhèn The Arousing, Thunder

The first [nine], undivided, shows its subject free from all insincerity. His advance will be accompanied with good fortune.

The first line is strong; at the commencement of the inner trigram denoting movement, the action of its subject will very much characterize all the action set forth, and will itself be fortunate.
