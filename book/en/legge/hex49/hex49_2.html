革 Gé / Revolution (Molting)

above:Duì The Joyous, Lake
below:Lí The Clinging, Flame

The second [six], divided, shows its subject making his changes after some time has passed. Action taken will be fortunate. There will be no error.

Line 2, though weak, is in its correct place. It is in the center also of the trigram Lí, signifying brightness and intelligence, and has a proper correlate in the strong 5. Let its subject take action in the way of change.
