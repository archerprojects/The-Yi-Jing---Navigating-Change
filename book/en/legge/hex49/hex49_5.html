革 Gé / Revolution (Molting)

above:Duì The Joyous, Lake
below:Lí The Clinging, Flame

The fifth [nine], undivided, shows the great man (producing his changes) as the tiger (does when he) changes (his stripes). Before he divines (and proceeds to action), faith has been reposed in him.

Line 5 has every quality proper to 'the lord of the hexagram', and his action will be in every way beneficial. He is symbolled by the tiger; and the changes which he makes by the bright stripes of the tiger when he has changed his coat.
