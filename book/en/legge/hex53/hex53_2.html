漸 Jiàn / Development (Gradual Progress)

above:Xùn The Gentle, Wind
below:Gèn Keeping Still, Mountain

The second [six], divided, shows the geese gradually approaching the large rocks, where they eat and drink joyfully at ease. There will be good fortune.

The geese have advanced in line 2, and so has the officer, though he is not mentioned. The line is weak or humble, and central, and has a proper correlate in 5. Hence comes the good auspice.
