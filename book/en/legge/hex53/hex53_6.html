漸 Jiàn / Development (Gradual Progress)

above:Xùn The Gentle, Wind
below:Gèn Keeping Still, Mountain

The sixth [nine], undivided, shows the geese gradually advanced to the large heights (beyond). Their feathers can be used as ornaments. There will be good fortune.

The subject of line 6 has reached the top of the hexagram. There is no more advance for him; and he has no correlate. But he may still do some good work for the state, and verify the auspice derived from the ornamental plumes of the geese.
