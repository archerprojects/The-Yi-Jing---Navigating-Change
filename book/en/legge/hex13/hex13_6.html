同人 Tóng Rén / Fellowship with Men

above: Qián The Creative, Heaven
below: Lí The Clinging, Flame

The topmost [nine], undivided, (shows the representative of) the union of men in the suburbs. There will be no occasion for repentance.

The union reaches to all within the suburbs, and is not yet universal; but still there is no cause for repentance.
