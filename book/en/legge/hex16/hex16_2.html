豫 Yù / Enthusiasm

above:Zhèn The Arousing, Thunder
below:Kūn The Receptive, Earth

The second [six], divided, shows one who is firm as a rock. (He sees a thing) without waiting until it has come to pass. With his firm correctness there will be good fortune.

Line 2, though weak, is in its correct position, the center, moreover, of the lower trigram. Quietly and firmly its subject is able to abide in his place, and exercise a far-seeing discrimination. All is indicative of good fortune.
