豫 Yù / Enthusiasm

above:Zhèn The Arousing, Thunder
below:Kūn The Receptive, Earth

The third [six], divided, shows one looking up (for favors) while he indulges in the feeling of pleasure and satisfaction. If he would understand! If he be late in doing so, there will indeed be occasion for repentance.

Line 3 is weak, and in an odd place. Immediately below line 4, its subject keeps looking up to the lord of the figure, and depends on him, thinking of doing nothing, but how to enjoy himself. The consequence will be as described, unless he speedily change.
