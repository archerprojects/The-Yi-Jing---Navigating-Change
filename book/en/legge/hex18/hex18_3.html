蠱 Gǔ / Work on What Has Been Spoiled [Decay]

above:Gèn Keeping Still, Mountain
below:Xùn The Gentle, Wind

The third [nine], undivided, shows (a son) dealing with the troubles caused by his father. There may be some small occasion for repentance, but there will not be any great error.

Line 3 is strong, and not central, so that its subject might well go to excess in his efforts. But this tendency is counteracted by the line's place in the trigram Xùn, often denoting lowly submission.
