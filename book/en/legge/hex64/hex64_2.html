未濟 Wèi Jì / Before Completion

above: Lí The Clinging, Flame
below: Kǎn The Abysmal, Water

The second [nine], undivided, shows its subject dragging back his (carriage-)wheel. With firmness and correctness there will be good fortune.

The subject of line 2, strong, and in the center, is able to repress himself, and keep back his carriage from advancing; and there is good fortune.
