未濟 Wèi Jì / Before Completion

above: Lí The Clinging, Flame
below: Kǎn The Abysmal, Water

The fifth [six], divided, shows its subject by firm correctness obtaining good fortune and having no occasion for repentance. (We see in him) the brightness of a superior man and the possession of sincerity. There will be good fortune.

Line 5 is weak, in an odd place. But its subject is the ruler, humble and supported by the subject of the strong 2; and hence the auspice is very good.
