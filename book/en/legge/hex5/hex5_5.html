需 Xū / Waiting (Nourishment)

above: Kǎn The Abysmal, Water
below: Qián The Creative, Heaven

The fifth [nine], undivided, shows its subject waiting amidst the appliances of a feast. Through his firmness and correctness there will be good fortune.

Line 5 is strong and central, and in its correct place, being that of honor. All good qualities therefore belong to the subject of it, who has triumphed, and with firmness will triumph still more.
