履 Lǚ / Treading [Conduct]

above: Qián The Creative, Heaven
below: Duì The Joyous, Lake

The topmost [nine], undivided, tells us to look at (the whole course) that is trodden and examine the presage which that gives. If it be complete and without failure, there will be great good fortune.

What is said on line 6 is good, but is only a truism. The whole course has been shown; if every step has been right and appropriate, the issue will be good.
