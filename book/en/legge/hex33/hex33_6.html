遯 Dùn / Retreat

above: Qián The Creative, Heaven
below: Gèn Keeping Still, Mountain

The sixth [nine], undivided, shows its subject retiring in a noble way. It will be advantageous in every way.

Line 6 is strong, and with no correlate to detain it in 3. Its subject vigorously and happily carries out the idea of the hexagram.
