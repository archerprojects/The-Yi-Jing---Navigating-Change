遯 Dùn / Retreat

above: Qián The Creative, Heaven
below: Gèn Keeping Still, Mountain

Dùn indicates successful progress (in its circumstances). To a small extent it will (still) be advantageous to be firm and correct.

Overall Meaning

Retreat, the symbol of regression

Dùn is the hexagram of the sixth month; the yin influence is represented by two weak lines, and has made good its footing in the year. The figure thus suggested to King Wén the growth of small and unprincipled men in the state, before whose advance superior men were obliged to retire. This is the theme of his essay, - how, 'when small men multiply and increase in power, the necessity of time requires superior men to withdraw before them.' Yet the auspice of Dùn is not all bad. By firm correctness the threatened evil may be arrested to a small extent.
