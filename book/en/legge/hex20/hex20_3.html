觀 Guān / Contemplation (View)

above:The Gentle, Wind
below:Kūn The Receptive, Earth

The third [six], divided, shows one looking at (the course of) his own life, to advance or recede (accordingly).

Line 3, at the top of the lower trigram Kūn, and weak, must belong to a subject of utmost docility, and will wish to act only according to the exigency of time and circumstances.
