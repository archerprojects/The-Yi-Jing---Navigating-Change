觀 Guān / Contemplation (View)

above:The Gentle, Wind
below:Kūn The Receptive, Earth

The first [six], divided, shows the looking of a lad; not blameable in men of inferior rank, but matter for regret in superior men.

Line 1 is weak, and in the lowest place, improper also for it; - the symbol of a thoughtless lad, who cannot see far, and takes only superficial views.
