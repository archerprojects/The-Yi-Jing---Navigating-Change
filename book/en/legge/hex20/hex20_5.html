觀 Guān / Contemplation (View)

above:The Gentle, Wind
below:Kūn The Receptive, Earth

The fifth [nine], undivided, shows the subject contemplating his own life(-course). A superior man, he will (thus) fall into no error.

Line 5 is strong, and in the place of the ruler. He is a superior man, but this does not relieve him from the duty of self-contemplation or examination.
