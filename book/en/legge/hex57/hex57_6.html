The sixth [nine], undivided, shows the representative of penetration beneath a couch, having lost the axe with which he executed his decisions. However firm and correct he may (try to) be, there will be evil.

The evil that paragraph 6 concludes with would arise from the quality of Xùn being carried to excess. I have followed the Kāngxī editors in adopting a change of one character in the received Text.
