大有 Dà Yǒu / Possession in Great Measure

above:Lí The Clinging, Flame
below:Qián The Creative, Heaven

The topmost [nine], undivided, shows its subject with the help accorded to him from Heaven. There will be good fortune; advantage in every respect.

Even the topmost line takes its character from 5. The strength of its subject is still tempered, and Heaven gives its approval.
