大有 Dà Yǒu / Possession in Great Measure

above:Lí The Clinging, Flame
below:Qián The Creative, Heaven

In the second [nine], undivided, we have a large wagon with its load. In whatever direction advance is made, there will be no error.

The strong line 2 has its proper correlate in line 5, the ruler of the figure, and will use its strength in subordination to his humility. Hence the symbolism.
