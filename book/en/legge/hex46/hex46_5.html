升 Shēng / Pushing Upward

above:Kūn The Receptive, Earth
below:Xùn The Gentle, Wind

The fifth [six], divided, shows its subject firmly correct, and therefore enjoying good fortune. He ascends the stairs (with all due ceremony).

In line 5 the advance has reached the highest point of dignity, and firm correctness is especially called for. 'Ascending the steps of a stair' may intimate, as Zhū Xī says, the ease of advance; or according to others (the Kāngxī editors among them), its ceremonious manner.
