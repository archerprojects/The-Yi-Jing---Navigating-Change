損 Sǔn / Decrease

above:Gèn Keeping Still, Mountain
below:Duì The Joyous, Lake

The first [nine], undivided, shows its subject suspending his own affairs and hurrying away (to help the subject of the fourth line). He will commit no error, but let him consider how far he should contribute of what is his (for the other).

Line 1 is strong, and its correlate in 4 is weak. Its subject will wish to help the subject of 4; but will not leave anything of his own undone in doing so. Nor will he diminish of his own for the other without due deliberation.
