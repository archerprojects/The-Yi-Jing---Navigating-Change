復 Fù / Return (The Turning Point)

above:Kūn The Receptive, Earth
below:Zhèn The Arousing, Thunder

The fourth [six], divided, shows its subject moving right in the center (among those represented by the other divided lines), and yet returning alone (to his proper path).

Line 4 has its proper correlate in 1; different from all the other weak lines; and its course is different accordingly.
