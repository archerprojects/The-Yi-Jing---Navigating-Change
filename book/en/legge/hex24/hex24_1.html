復 Fù / Return (The Turning Point)

above:Kūn The Receptive, Earth
below:Zhèn The Arousing, Thunder

The first [nine], undivided, shows its subject returning (from an error) of no great extent, which would not proceed to anything requiring repentance. There will be great good fortune.

The subject of line 1 is of course the undivided line, meaning here, says Chéng Zǐ, 'the way of the superior man'. There must have been some deviation from that, or 'returning' could not be spoken of.
