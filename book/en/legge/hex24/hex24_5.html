復 Fù / Return (The Turning Point)

above:Kūn The Receptive, Earth
below:Zhèn The Arousing, Thunder

The fifth [six], divided, shows the noble return (of its subject). There will be no ground for repentance.

Line 5 is in the central place of honor, and the middle line of Kūn, denoting docility. Hence its auspice.
