小畜 Xiǎo Xù / The Taming Power of the Small

above: Xùn The Gentle, Wind
below: Qián The Creative, Heaven

The second [nine], undivided, shows its subject, by the attraction (of the former line), returning (to the proper course). There will be good fortune.

Line 2 is also strong, and though in an even place is not appropriate to it, that place being central, its subjects will make common cause with the subject of line 1; and there will be good fortune.
