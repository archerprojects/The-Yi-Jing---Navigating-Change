小畜 Xiǎo Xù / The Taming Power of the Small

above: Xùn The Gentle, Wind
below: Qián The Creative, Heaven

The fourth [six], divided, shows its subject possessed of sincerity. The danger of bloodshed is thereby averted, and his (ground for) apprehension dismissed. There will be no mistake.

The subject of line 4, one weak line against all the strong lines of the hexagram, might well expect wounds, and feel apprehension in trying to restrain the others; but it is in its proper place; it is the first line also of Xùn, whose attribute is docile flexibility. The strong lines are moved to sympathy and help, and 'there is no mistake'.
