小畜 Xiǎo Xù / The Taming Power of the Small

above: Xùn The Gentle, Wind
below: Qián The Creative, Heaven

The first [nine], undivided, shows its subject returning and pursuing his own course. What mistake should he fall into? There will be good fortune.

Line 1 is undivided, the first line of Qián, occupying its proper place. Its subject, therefore, notwithstanding the check of line 4, resumes his movement, and will act according to his strong nature, and go forward.
