中孚 Zhōng Fú / Inner Truth

above: Xùn The Gentle, Wind
below: Duì The Joyous, Lake

The fifth [nine], undivided, shows its subject perfectly sincere and linking (others) to him in closest union. There will be no error.

Line 5 is strong and central, in the rulers' place. Its subject must be the sage on the throne, whose sincerity will go forth and bind all in union with himself.
