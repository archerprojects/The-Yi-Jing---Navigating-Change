大壯 Dà Zhuàng / The Power of the Great

above: Zhèn The Arousing, Thunder
below: Qián The Creative, Heaven

The second [nine], undivided, shows that with firm correctness there will be good fortune.

Line 2 is strong, but the strength is tempered by its being in an even place, instead of being excited by it, as might be feared. Then the place is that in the center. With firm correctness there will be good fortune.
