大壯 Dà Zhuàng / The Power of the Great

above: Zhèn The Arousing, Thunder
below: Qián The Creative, Heaven

The fourth [nine], undivided, shows (a case in which) firm correctness that leads to good fortune, and occasion for repentance disappears. (We see) the fence opened without the horns being entangled. The strength is like that in the wheel-spokes of a large wagon.

Line 4 is still strong, but in the place of a weak line; and this gives occasion to the cautions with which the symbolism commences. The subject of the line going forward thus cautiously, his strength will produce good effects, such as are described.
