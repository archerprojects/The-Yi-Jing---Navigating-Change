艮 Gèn / Keeping Still, Mountain

above:Gèn Keeping Still, Mountain
below:Gèn Keeping Still, Mountain

The fifth [six], divided, shows its subject keeping his jawbones at rest, so that his words are (all) orderly. Occasion for repentance will disappear.

The place of the weak fifth line is not proper for it; and this accounts for the mention of its subject 'repenting', for which, however, there is not occasion.
