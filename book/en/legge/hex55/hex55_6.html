豐 Fēng / Abundance [Fullness]

above: Zhèn The Arousing, Thunder
below: Lí The Clinging, Flame

The topmost [six], divided, shows its subject with his house made large, but only serving as a screen to his household. When he looks at his door, it is still, and there is nobody about it. For three years no one is to be seen. There will be evil.

All the conditions of line 6 are unfavorable, and its subject is left to himself without any helpers. He is isolated for long, and undone. The issue is only evil.
