豐 Fēng / Abundance [Fullness]

above: Zhèn The Arousing, Thunder
below: Lí The Clinging, Flame

The third [nine], undivided, shows its subject with an (additional) screen of a large and thick banner, through which at midday he can see (the small) Mei star. (In the darkness) he breaks his right arm; but there will be no error.

Line 3 is strong, in its proper place. It is the last line moreover of the trigram of brightness. All these conditions are favorable to the employment of its subject; but its correlate is the weak 6, which is at the extremity of the trigram of movement. There is no more power therefore in 6, and the subject of 3 has no one to cooperate with him. His symbolism and auspice are worse than those of 2; but his own proper goodness and capacity will save him from error. Mei is a small star in or near the bushel.
