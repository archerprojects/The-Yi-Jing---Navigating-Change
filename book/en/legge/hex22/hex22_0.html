Bǐ indicates that there should be free course (in what it denotes). There will be little advantage (however) if allowed to advance (and take the lead).

Overall Meaning

Ornamental, the symbol of model

The character of Bǐ is the symbol of what is ornamental and of the act of adorning. As there is ornament in nature, so should there be in society; but its place is secondary to that of what is substantial. This is the view of King Wén in his Tuàn. The symbolism of the separate lines is sometimes fantastic.
