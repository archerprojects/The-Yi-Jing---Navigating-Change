坤 Kūn / The Receptive

above: Kūn The Receptive, Earth
below: Kūn The Receptive, Earth

In the third [six], divided, (shows its subject) keeping his excellence under restraint, but firmly maintaining it. If he should have occasion to engage in the king's service, though he will not claim the success (for himself), he will bring affairs to a good issue.

To keep his excellence under restraint, as in paragraph 3, is the part of a minister or officer, seeking not his own glory, but that of his ruler.
