益 Yì / Increase

above:Xùn The Gentle, Wind
below:Zhèn The Arousing, Thunder

In the sixth [nine], undivided, we see one to whose increase none will contribute, while many will seek to assail him. He observes no regular rule in the ordering of his heart. There will be evil.

Line 6 is also strong; but it should be weak. Occupying the topmost place of the figure, its subject will concentrate his powers on the increase of himself, and not think of benefitting those below him; and the consequence will be as described.
