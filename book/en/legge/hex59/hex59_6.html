渙 Huàn / Dispersion [Dissolution]

above: Xùn The Gentle, Wind
below: Kǎn The Abysmal, Water

The topmost [nine], undivided, shows its subject disposing of (what may be called) its bloody wounds, and going and separating himself from its anxious fears. There will be no error.

Line 6 is occupied by a strong line, which has a proper correlate in 3; but 3 is at the top of the trigram of peril. The subject of 6 hurries away from association with the subject of it, but does so in the spirit of the hexagram, so that there is no blame or error attaching to it.
