比 Bǐ / Holding Together [Union]

above: Kǎn The Abysmal, Water
below: Kūn The Receptive, Earth

In the topmost [six], divided, we see one seeking union and attachment without having taken the first step (to such an end). There will be evil.

A weak line being in the sixth place, which is appropriate to it, its subject is supposed to be trying to promote union among and with the subjects of the lines below. It is too late. The time is past. Hence it is symbolized as 'without a head', that is, as not having taken the first step, from which its action should begin, and go on to the end.
