兌 Duì / The Joyous, Lake

The topmost [six], divided, shows the pleasure of its subject in leading and attracting others.

The symbolism of paragraph 6 is akin to that of 3, though no positive auspice is expressed. The subject of line 3 attracts others around itself for the sake of pleasure; the subject of this leads them to follow himself in quest of it.
