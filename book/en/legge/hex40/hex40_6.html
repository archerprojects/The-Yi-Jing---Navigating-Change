解 Xiè / Deliverance

above: Zhèn The Arousing, Thunder
below: Kǎn The Abysmal, Water

In the sixth [six], divided, we see a feudal prince (with his bow) shooting at a falcon at the top of a high wall, and hitting it. (The effect of his action) will be in every way advantageous.

Line 6 is the highest line in the figure, but not the place of the ruler. Hence it appears as occupied by a feudal duke, who carries out the idea of the figure against small men, according to the symbolism employed.
