睽 Kuí / Opposition

above: Lí The Clinging, Flame
below: Duì The Joyous, Lake

The second [nine], undivided, shows its subject happening to meet with his lord in a by-passage. There will be no error.

Line 5 is weak, and its subject is the proper correlate of the strong 2. They might meet openly; but for the separation and disunion that mark the time. A casual, as it were a stolen, interview, as in a by-lane or passage, however will be useful, and may lead on to a better understanding.
