蹇 39

彖传

蹇，难也，险在前也。见险而能止，知矣哉！蹇利西南，往得中也；不利东北，其道穷也。利见大人，往有功也。当位贞吉，以正邦也。蹇之时用大矣哉！

Jiǎn denotes difficulty. There is (the trigram expressive of) perilousness in front. When one, seeing the peril, can arrest his steps (in accordance with the significance of the lower tri. gram), is he not wise? (The language of) Jiǎn, that 'advantage will be found in the south-west,' refers to the (strong fifth line) advanced and in the central place. That 'there will be no advantage in the north-east,' intimates that the way (of dealing with the Jiǎn state) is exhausted. That 'it will be advantageous to see the great man,' intimates that advance will lead to achievement. That the places (of the different lines after the first) are those appropriate to them indicates firm correctness and good fortune, with which the regions (of the kingdom) are brought to their normal state. Great indeed is the work to be done in the time of Jiǎn!

象传（大象）

山上有水，蹇；君子以反身修德。

(The trigram representing) a mountain, and above it that for water, form Jiǎn. The superior man, in accordance with this, turns round (and examines) himself, and cultivates his virtue.

序卦

《睽》者，乖也。乖必有难，故受之以《蹇》。

Kuí denotes misunderstanding and division; and such a state is sure to give rise to difficulties and complications. Kuí therefore is followed by Jiǎn.

杂卦

《蹇》，难也。

Hard toil and danger have in Jiǎn their home.
