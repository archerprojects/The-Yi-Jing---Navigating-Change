漸 53

彖传

渐之进也，女归吉也。进得位，往有功也。进以正，可以正邦也。其位刚，得中也。止而巽，动不穷也。

The advance indicated by Jiàn is (like) the marrying of a young lady which is attended by good fortune. (The lines) as they advance get into their correct places: - this indicates the achievements of a successful progress. The advance is made according to correctness: - (the subject of the hexagram) might rectify his country. Among the places (of the hexagram) we see the strong undivided line in the centre. 'In (the attributes of) restfulness and flexible penetration we have (the assurance of) an (onward) movement that is inexhaustible.

象传（大象）

山上有木，渐；君子以居贤德，善俗。

(The trigram representing) a mountain and above it that for a tree form Jiàn. The superior man, in accordance with this, attains to and maintains his extraordinary virtue, and makes the manners of the people good.

序卦

《艮》者，止也。物不可以终止，故受之以《渐》。

Gèn gives the idea of arresting or stopping. Things cannot be kept for ever in a state of repression, and hence Gèn is followed by Jiàn.

杂卦

《渐》，女归待男行也。

In Jiàn we see a bride who will delayTo move until the bridegroom takes his way.
