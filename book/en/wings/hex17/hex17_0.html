隨 17

彖传

随，刚来而下柔，动而说，随。大亨贞，无咎，而天下随时，随时之义大矣哉！

In Suí we see the strong (trigram) come and place itself under the weak; we see (in the two) the attributes of movement and pleasure: - this gives (the idea of) Suí. 'There will be great progress and success; and through firm correctness no error:' - all under heaven will be found following at such a time. Great indeed are the time and significance indicated in Suí.

象传（大象）

泽中有雷，随；君子以向晦入宴息。

(The trigram for the waters of) a marsh and (that for) thunder (hidden) in the midst of it form Suí. The superior man in accordance with this, when it is getting towards dark, enters (his house) and rests.

序卦

豫必有随，故受之以《随》。

Where such complacency is awakened, (he who causes it) is sure to have followers (Suí).

杂卦

《随》，无故也。
