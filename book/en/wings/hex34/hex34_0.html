大壯 34

彖传

大壮，大者壮也。刚以动，故壮。大壮利贞；大者正也。正大而天地之情可见矣！

In Dà Zhuàng we see that which is great becoming strong. We have the (trigram) denoting strength directing that which denotes movement, and hence (the whole) is expressive of vigour. 'Dà Zhuàng indicates that it will be advantageous to be firm and correct:' - that which is great (should be) correct. Given correctness and greatness (in their highest degree), and the character and tendencies of heaven and earth can be seen.

象传（大象）

雷在天上，大壮；君子以非礼勿履。

(The trigram representing) heaven and above it that for thunder form Dà Zhuàng. The superior man, in accordance with this, does not take a step which is not according to propriety.

序卦

《遯》者，退也。物不可以终遯，故受之以《大壮》。

Dùn denotes withdrawing. Things cannot be for ever withdrawn; and hence Dùn is succeeded by Dà Zhuàng.

杂卦

《大壮》则止，《遯》则退也。

Dà Zhuàng stops here as right; withdraws Dùn there.
